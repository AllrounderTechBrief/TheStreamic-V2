Newsroom

Newsroom systems, automation, and editorial workflows

Newsroom technology powers the editorial workflows and content management systems that drive modern journalism and broadcast news operations. Our Newsroom section covers the comprehensive ecosystem of tools enabling efficient story creation, collaborative editing, and multi-platform publishing. We track developments in newsroom computer systems (NRCS) from industry leaders like Avid iNews, Ross OverDrive, and Octopus Newsroom, examining how these platforms integrate with rundown automation, prompter systems, and graphics workflows. From exploring cloud-based newsroom solutions enabling remote collaboration to analyzing mobile journalism (MoJo) tools, field production systems, and social media integration platforms, our coverage helps broadcast journalists, news directors, and technology managers navigate the evolving landscape. We feature detailed analyses of assignment desk software, media asset management specialized for news, automated transcription services, and AI-powered content production tools. Whether you're implementing a new NRCS, optimizing editorial workflows, or evaluating tools for multi-platform story distribution, our Newsroom section delivers practical insights and technical depth essential for modern news operations.

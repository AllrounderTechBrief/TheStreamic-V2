Editor's Desk

Streaming

OTT platforms, video delivery, and streaming technology

Streaming technology has fundamentally transformed how audiences consume media, creating new business models and technical challenges for content providers worldwide. Our Streaming section delivers authoritative coverage of OTT platforms, video delivery infrastructure, and the evolving protocols that power everything from live sports broadcasts to on-demand entertainment services. We analyze developments in adaptive bitrate streaming (HLS, DASH), low-latency protocols (CMAF, LL-HLS, WebRTC), and emerging standards that reduce buffering while improving quality of experience. Our coverage spans CDN architectures, edge computing strategies, DRM implementations, and monetization approaches including AVOD, SVOD, and hybrid models. We track platform providers like Brightcove, Kaltura, and JW Player, examine infrastructure solutions from Akamai, Cloudflare, and Fastly, and explore how major streamers like Netflix, Disney+, and Amazon Prime Video are innovating in encoding, personalization, and global delivery. Whether you're launching an OTT service, optimizing existing streaming workflows, or evaluating new delivery technologies, our Streaming section provides the technical depth and strategic insights essential for success in today's competitive landscape.
